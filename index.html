About

Trained in policy.
Wired for communication.

I believe analysis is only as powerful as its ability to reach the people who can act on it — so I write for El Comercio, speak in hundreds of media appearances each year, and build tools that make Peru's data accessible to anyone.

Master in Public Policy from the University of Chicago's Harris School (graduated with honors, double certification in Data Analytics and Policy Analysis). BS in Economics from Universidad del Pacífico. Former consultant at ECLAC / CEPAL (United Nations).
